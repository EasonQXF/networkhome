## SVG有趣的例子
- 鼠标停放在div中颜色会渐变
<head>
<style> 
div
{
width:100px;
height:100px;
background:red;
animation:my 5s;
}

@keyframes my
{
from {background:red;}
to {background:blue;}
}



</style>
</head>
<body>

<div></div>

<p>渐变色</p>

</body>
